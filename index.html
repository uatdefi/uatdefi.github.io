ABOUT TOKEN

The UAT Finance token UAT is a BEP20 token that originally launched on Binance Smart Chain. You can store it in any wallet that supports the BSC network and send / receive UAT like any other coin.

The price of the UAT token is fixed by the contract and only grows by 0.2% daily (by 6% every month). The initial price is 0.1 BNB per 1 UAT.

The token can be obtained only through the staking procedure in one of the pools or through the referral program. You can swap UAT to BNB at any time at exchange`s section.

UAT is used to enter a lottery on WineDeFi. Each lottery session takes 12 hours. 10% of each collection of rewards in the pools goes towards buying tickets. After 12 hours, the contract chooses the winner and pays him 90% of the game`s pot.
